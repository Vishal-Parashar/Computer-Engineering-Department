Computer Engineering is a very vast discipline. The course trains the students of the basics and different aspects of computer engineering. It includes fundamentals of computer systems, computer organization, networking, database management systems, digital electronics, operating system, programming languages, web designing, multimedia applications, concepts of software engineering as well as hardware engineering.

The Computer Engineering Professionals can work as a computer programmer, systems analyst, software developer, hardware engineer, IT consultant, system engineer, IT specialist, networking engineer, database administrator, web developer, mobility tester, programmer, software tester and execute various other job profiles.Here at Govt. Polytechnic Sonipat, We believe in approach to Teaching- Learning, coupled with practical experience gained during Industrial training in reputed organization, equips our students to handle the challenges posed by the software industry due to vast changes in trends and technologies.

Computer Department has been awarded as “The Best Department” at Govt. Polytechnic Sonipat and has been honored by Hon’ble Vice Chancellor, Deen Bandhu Chotu Ram University of Science &Technology, Murthal, Sonipat in Annual Prize Distribution Function 2022.
